Matilde Perotti

PhD Student
Department of Social and Political Sciences, Bocconi University

Hi, and thanks for visiting my webpage! I am a PhD student in the Department of Social and Political Sciences at Bocconi University. My scholarship is funded by FOSSR – Fostering Open Science in Social Science Research, a project supported by the Italian National Recovery and Resilience Plan (PNRR) – NextGenerationEU under Grant Agreement MUR IR0000008 (fossr.eu). As part of FOSSR, I work within the Italian team of the Generations and Gender Survey, contributing to the implementation of the Italian wave currently in progress.

My research focuses on two main areas. First, I study the life course of young adults across Europe, analyzing how school experiences and social environments shape their transition to adulthood. Second, drawing on my experience with grassroots NGOs, I investigate border enforcement practices and migration policies in Europe.
