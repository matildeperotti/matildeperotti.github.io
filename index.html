Matilde Perotti

PhD Student
Department of Social and Political Sciences, Bocconi University

Hi, and thanks for visiting my webpage! I am a PhD student in the Department of Social and Political Sciences at Bocconi University. My scholarship is funded by FOSSR – Fostering Open Science in Social Science Research , a project supported by the Italian National Recovery and Resilience Plan (PNRR) – NextGenerationEU. As part of FOSSR, I work within the Italian team of the Generations and Gender Survey .

My research interests lie at the intersection of political sociology, demography, and migration. I study how ethnically diverse environments in contemporary Europe shape socio-demographic outcomes, including attitudes toward migrants and gender, as well as fertility and family formation. In my work, I use both observational and experimental methods.
